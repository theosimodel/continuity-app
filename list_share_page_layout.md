## 1. Purpose of the Share Page

A List share page exists to:

- Communicate **taste and intent**
- Provide a **clear reading path**
- Invite readers to **begin their own Continuity**

It must work equally well for:
- Logged-out users
- First-time visitors
- Returning signed-in users

The page should feel like:
> *An editorial handoff, not a social profile.*

## 5. Comic List (Primary Surface)

### Each Comic Card Must Include

- Cover image

## 7. Share Page Privacy Rules

List share pages must:

- Never expose private notes
- Never show reading timestamps
- Never show user activity history
- Never auto-add comics

A shared List is **a recommendation, not a commitment**.
